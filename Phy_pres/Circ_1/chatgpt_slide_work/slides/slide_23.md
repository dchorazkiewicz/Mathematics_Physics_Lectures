# 23. Choosing Current Directions

## Goal

Show that assumed current directions are arbitrary.

## Slide layout

Title:

**Choosing Current Directions**

Two panels side by side.

Left panel:

**Assumed direction**

Draw a simple circuit with current arrow (I) assumed clockwise.

Right panel:

**Negative result**

Draw the same circuit, but show:

[
I=-0.4A
]

Then show a second arrow in the opposite direction labeled:

**actual direction**

Bottom:

**A negative result means the real current flows in the opposite direction**

## Visual details

Use the same circuit in both panels. Make the reversal very clear.

## Avoid

Do not explain sign conventions in long text.
