# 6. Voltage as Potential Difference

## Goal

Introduce voltage as a difference of electric potential.

## Slide layout

Title:

**Voltage as Potential Difference**

Left half: energy-height analogy.

Draw two points:

* (A) higher
* (B) lower

Right half: simple circuit with a battery and resistor.

Bottom:

**Voltage = potential difference**

## Visual details

The left side may use a subtle hill/height metaphor, but keep it symbolic and clean. The right side should be a simple rectangular circuit: battery on the left, resistor on the right, current arrow around the loop.

## Avoid

Do not define voltage through work yet. Keep this intuitive.
