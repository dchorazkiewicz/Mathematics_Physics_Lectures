# 11. Microscopic Origin of Resistance

## Goal

Explain resistance through scattering of electrons on the lattice.

## Slide layout

Title:

**Microscopic Origin of Resistance**

Two panels side by side.

Left:

**Cooler lattice**

Right:

**Warmer lattice**

Bottom:

**More lattice vibrations → more scattering → larger resistance**

## Visual details

Left panel: positive ions almost fixed, small vibration marks, electron path fairly smooth.

Right panel: positive ions with larger vibration halos, electron path more broken, more collision points.

Electrons should remain blue. Heat/vibration should be red/orange.

## Avoid

Do not make the slide look like thermal physics only. Keep the relation to current visible.
