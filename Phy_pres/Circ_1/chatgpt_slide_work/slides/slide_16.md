# 16. Joule Heating

## Goal

Show the Joule effect in a resistor.

## Slide layout

Title:

**Joule Heating**

Center: resistor in a circuit, glowing red/orange.

Draw current (I) through the resistor.

Label resistor (R).

Bottom center: large formula:

[
P=I^2R
]

Lower right corner: small real-world icons:

* heater
* kettle
* fuse

## Visual details

The resistor should be the visual focus. Use red/orange glow and heat waves. The current arrows should remain clear.

## Avoid

Do not overload with many applications. Use only small icons.
