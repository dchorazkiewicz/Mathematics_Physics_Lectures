# 4. Random Motion and Drift

## Goal

Show that electron motion is mostly chaotic, but with a small average drift in an electric field.

## Slide layout

Title:

**Random Motion and Drift**

Two panels side by side.

Left panel title:

**No external field**

Right panel title:

**With external field**

Bottom sentence:

**Random motion + small drift = electric current**

## Visual details

Left panel: many blue electrons with short arrows in random directions.

Right panel: many blue electrons still have random arrows, but add a clear average drift direction. Add a large blue arrow labeled (E). Add one small label:

**drift velocity**

## Avoid

Do not show electrons as simply flying straight through the wire. The point is that drift is small compared to chaotic motion.
