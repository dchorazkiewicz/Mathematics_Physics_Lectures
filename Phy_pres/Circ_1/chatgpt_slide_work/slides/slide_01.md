# 1. What Is Electric Current?

## Goal

Introduce electric current as directed flow of electric charge.

## Slide layout

Title at the top left:

**What Is Electric Current?**

In the center, draw a simple horizontal conductor with charges moving through it in one clear direction.

Above the conductor, draw a large blue arrow:

**electric field (E)**

Below the conductor, draw a red/orange arrow:

**conventional current (I)**

At the bottom:

**Ordered motion of electric charges**

## Visual details

Draw the conductor as a gray metallic strip or cylinder. Charges should be blue dots with small minus signs. Use a few clean motion arrows so the idea is simple and readable. Keep this slide general and intuitive.

## Avoid

Do not show random thermal motion yet. Do not show microscopic lattice details. Do not use (I=\frac{dq}{dt}) yet.
