# 10. Electrical Resistance

## Goal

Show resistance as opposition to current.

## Slide layout

Title:

**Electrical Resistance**

Two panels side by side.

Left panel:

**Low resistance**

Right panel:

**High resistance**

Bottom:

**Resistance opposes current**

## Visual details

Low resistance: conductor with few obstacles, electrons move relatively smoothly, current arrow thick.

High resistance: conductor with many scattering/obstacle symbols, electron paths more broken, current arrow thinner.

Use the same voltage symbol in both panels to suggest comparison at equal voltage.
