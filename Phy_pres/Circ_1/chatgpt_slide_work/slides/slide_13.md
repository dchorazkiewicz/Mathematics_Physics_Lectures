# 13. Interpreting Ohm’s Law

## Goal

Show the qualitative meaning of (U=IR).

## Slide layout

Title:

**Interpreting Ohm’s Law**

Use three horizontal panels:

1. **Baseline**
2. **Higher voltage**
3. **Higher resistance**

Bottom:

**Increase voltage → current increases**
**Increase resistance → current decreases**

## Visual details

Panel 1: normal battery, resistor, medium current arrow.

Panel 2: larger battery or larger (U), same resistor, thicker current arrow.

Panel 3: same battery, larger resistor, thinner current arrow.

Use the same simple circuit in each panel.

## Avoid

Do not introduce new equations. The visual comparison is the point.
