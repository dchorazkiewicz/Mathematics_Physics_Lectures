# 8. Open and Closed Circuit

## Goal

Show that current needs a complete conducting path.

## Slide layout

Title:

**Open and Closed Circuit**

Two panels side by side.

Left panel:

**Open circuit**

Battery, wires, bulb, open switch. Bulb is off. No current arrows.

Label:

**No complete path**

Right panel:

**Closed circuit**

Same circuit, but switch closed. Bulb glows. Current arrows go around the full loop.

Label:

**Current flows**

Bottom:

**Current needs a complete conducting loop**

## Visual details

Use the same circuit geometry in both panels. Only the switch state and current arrows change.

## Avoid

Do not add equations.
