# 7. How a Battery Maintains Voltage

## Goal

Show that a battery separates charges and maintains potential difference.

## Slide layout

Title:

**How a Battery Maintains Voltage**

Center: simplified cross-section of a battery.

Left side: negative electrode.
Right side: positive electrode.
Inside: electrolyte.

Outside the battery, draw a wire with electron flow.

Inside the battery, draw ion motion and chemical separation.

Labels:

* **chemical reactions**
* **charge separation**
* **potential difference**

Bottom:

**A battery does not create charge — it separates charge**

## Visual details

Use a symbolic battery cross-section, not a realistic photograph. Show electrons outside the battery and ions inside. Keep labels short.

## Avoid

Do not add detailed chemistry. The point is the role of the battery in the circuit.
