# 9. Current Intensity

## Goal

Introduce current as charge passing through a cross-section per time.

## Slide layout

Title:

**Current Intensity**

Left two-thirds: horizontal conductor with a marked cross-section.

Draw charges passing through the cross-section.

Right one-third: large formula:

[
I=\frac{\Delta q}{\Delta t}
]

Below it, smaller:

[
1A=1C/s
]

Bottom:

**How much charge passes per second?**

## Visual details

Cross-section should be a clear vertical plane cutting the wire. Charges should pass through it with arrows. Mark:

[
\Delta q
]

and

[
\Delta t
]

## Avoid

Do not overload with many units or definitions.
